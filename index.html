Tile Wars

How To Play

Objective

Shoot the opposing player to win!

Rules

You can only move within tiles you own.

To claim a tile, shoot it!

To block your opponent's bullets, shoot them. When bullets collide, they are cancelled.

Each player has 6 bullets. One bullet will regenerate every 1 second.

Controls

Player 1:
Use the W, A, S, D keys to move around.
Use the Y, G, H, J keys to shoot.

Player 2:
Use the arrow keys to move around.
Use the 8, 4, 5, 6 keys to shoot.
